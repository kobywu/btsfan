Love Yourself: Answer
About the Albums

Love Yourself Series is composed of 3 albums Love Yourself: Her, Love Yourself: Tear and Love Yourself: Answer.

Love Yourself: Her was released on September 18, 2017 by Big Hit Entertainment. It includes 9 tracks with "DNA" as their leading single. Love Yourself: Her became the highest-charting Korean Pop album on the US Billboard 200 placing number 7. It also had the biggest sale week for a Korean Pop album.

Love Yourself: Tear was released on May 18, 2018 as the sequal of Love Yourself: Her. It includes 11 tracks with "Fake Love" as their leading single. This album debuted number one on the US Billboard 200 which is the highest-charting album a Korean artist has placed in the US and the Western Market. It surpassed thev records Love Yourself: Her created.

Love Yourself: Answer was the final album of the Love Yourself series released August 24, 2018. This is a repackage of the 2 pervious albums with 7 new songs and "Idol" as their leading single. In total there are 25 tracks. Love Yourself: Answer became the second time they debuted at number one in the US markets.

Tour
The Love Yourself World Tour promotes all 3 albums of the Love Yourself series beginning on August 25, 2018 in South Korea. BTS preformed in 12 countries with 42 shows. The tour was extended called BTS World Tour Love Yourself: Speak Yourself starting in Pasadena, California on May 4, 2019. The tour will be completed on July 14, 2019 in Shizuoka, Japan.
