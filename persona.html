Map of the Soul: Persona
About the Album
Map of the Soul: Persona was released on April 12, 2019 by Big Hit Entertainment. It is the follow up album of Love Yourself: Answer consists of 7 songs with Boy with Luv featuring U.S. artist Halsey. Map of Soul: Persona is the best selling album in the United States and Korea so far making BTS have 3 number-one albums in just year since The Beatles.
