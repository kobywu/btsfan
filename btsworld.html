BTS World
About the Albums

BTS World is the first mobile app that BTS has released on June 26, 2019 by Netmarble and Takeone Company Corp. The story line is that the player is their manager who is trying to prepare them for their debut. There is also seven different story lines for each BTS member that the player will go through such as helping Kim Namjun the detective find a young girl. A soundtrack album, "BTS World," was released for this app that features Charlie XCX, Juice Wrld and Zara Larsson. These songs plus their leading track "Heartbeat" are played throughout the app. This game makes the player feel like they are their actual manager with the option to "facetime" the members and do "phone calls" with their real voices and themselves.
